About the Graduate School of Biomedical Sciences

Training the next generation of biomedical scientists and problem solvers

Located in the heart of the UT Health San Antonio campus, the Graduate School of Biomedical Sciences provides a multidisciplinary learning environment for students to develop the knowledge, skills and creativity necessary to seek solutions to even the most challenging biomedical issues.

Because biomedicine rarely fits neatly into one field, our nationally recognized researchers and their trainees synergize the healing professions to conduct research which helps improve scientific knowledge and advance medical technology.
