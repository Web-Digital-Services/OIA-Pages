Make a Gift Now

Your support matters

At UT Health San Antonio, every gift tells a story of hope, healing and progress. Our mission is clear: to make lives better through exceptional education, groundbreaking research and compassionate care. Every day, our community, alumni and friends help turn this mission into reality. 

Your support drives discoveries that lead to new treatments for cancer, diabetes, Alzheimer’s and more. It opens doors for talented students to become the skilled health professionals our communities need and helps us expand resources and facilities to serve South Texas and beyond. 

This is an exciting time in our history. As we join with UTSA to create UT San Antonio—soon the third-largest research university in Texas—your partnership is more important than ever. 

Together, we can ensure South Texans have access to world-class care and prepare the next generation of healthcare leaders to serve with knowledge, integrity and compassion. Thank you for standing with us. 

Anamaria Repetti
Vice President and Chief Development Officer
