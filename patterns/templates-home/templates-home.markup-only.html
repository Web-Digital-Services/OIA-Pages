YOUR GIFT MATTERS

Innovation needs partners

Our mission is to make lives better. But we can’t do it alone. Every gift to UT Health is critical to our ability to heal, teach and innovate. From breakthrough research to compassionate care, your generosity helps create healthier futures for our families, our community, our nation and the world.

The philanthropic partnership of so many people like you is the foundation upon which we can educate the next generation of health care professionals and perform translational research. We are laser-focused on new and better prevention and treatment strategies for diseases prevalent in South Texas and providing comprehensive care right here, for those who need it most.

“UT Health San Antonio is dedicated to three missions: educating the next generation of physicians, investigating the causes and cures of disease and providing cutting edge medical care.”

Dean Robert Hromas, MD, FACP
