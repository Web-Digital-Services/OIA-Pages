Presidents Council

Be a part of our journey of compassion and good health.

Established in 1992, the President’s Council is composed of compassionate community members who make an annual contribution of $1,000 or more. The President’s Council has awarded more than $10.5 million to advance the health of our community and make countless lives better across the country.
